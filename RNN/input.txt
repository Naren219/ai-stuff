1. Classic Scrambled Eggs: Whisk 3 eggs with a pinch of salt and a splash of milk. Melt 1 tbsp butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat. Pour in eggs and stir gently and continuously with a spatula until soft curds form. Serve immediately.
2. Avocado Toast: Toast 2 slices of sourdough bread. Mash 1 ripe avocado with lemon juice, salt, and red pepper flakes. Spread over toast and top with a poached or fried egg.
3. Overnight Oats: In a jar, mix 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1/2 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp chia seeds, and 1 tsp maple syrup. Refrigerate overnight. Top with berries before eating.
4. Banana Pancakes: Mash 1 ripe banana and whisk with 2 eggs and a dash of cinnamon (no flour needed). Pour small circles of batter onto a greased skillet over medium heat. Flip carefully when bubbles appear.
5. Grilled Cheese Sandwich: Butter the outside of two slices of bread. Place one slice butter-side down in a pan, add 2 slices of cheddar cheese, and top with the second slice (butter-side up). Grill until golden brown on both sides.
6. Caprese Salad: Slice fresh mozzarella and tomatoes into rounds. Arrange on a plate, alternating slices. Tuck in fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil; season with salt and pepper.
7. Chicken Quesadilla: Place a flour tortilla in a hot pan. Sprinkle with shredded Mexican cheese blend and cooked diced chicken. Fold in half. Cook until golden and cheese melts, flipping once.
8. Tuna Salad: Drain 1 can of tuna. Mix with 2 tbsp mayonnaise, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, diced celery, salt, and pepper. Serve over greens or on toasted bread.
9. Garlic Butter Shrimp: Sauté 1 lb peeled shrimp in 2 tbsp butter and 3 minced garlic cloves over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes until pink. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and parsley.
10. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio: Cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water. In a pan, sauté 4 sliced garlic cloves and red pepper flakes in 1/4 cup olive oil. Toss pasta in the oil, adding pasta water to create a sauce.
11. Oven-Roasted Salmon: Place salmon fillets on a foil-lined baking sheet. Rub with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and dill. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes.
12. Basic Vinaigrette: Whisk together 3 parts olive oil to 1 part vinegar (balsamic or apple cider). Add 1 tsp Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Shake well.
13. Guacamole: Mash 3 avocados. Mix in 1/2 diced onion, 2 chopped Roma tomatoes, 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, 1 minced jalapeño, and juice of 1 lime. Salt to taste.
14. Tomato Basil Soup: Sauté onions and garlic. Add 2 cans crushed tomatoes and 2 cups vegetable broth. Simmer for 20 minutes. Blend until smooth. Stir in fresh basil and a splash of cream.
15. Beef Tacos: Brown 1 lb ground beef. Drain fat. Add 1 packet taco seasoning and 1/4 cup water. Simmer until thickened. Serve in taco shells with lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
16. Vegetable Stir-Fry: Sauté broccoli, bell peppers, and carrots in oil over high heat. Add soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and a teaspoon of sesame oil. Serve over rice.
17. Baked Potato: Scrub a russet potato, poke holes with a fork, and rub with oil and salt. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 1 hour. Slice open and fluff.
18. Greek Salad: Combine chopped cucumber, tomato, red onion, Kalamata olives, and cubes of feta cheese. Dress with olive oil, oregano, and lemon juice.
19. Pan-Seared Steak: Season a ribeye heavily with salt and pepper. Sear in a screaming hot cast-iron skillet with oil for 3-4 minutes per side. Baste with butter, garlic, and thyme in the final minute. Rest for 5 minutes.
20. Pesto Sauce: Blend 2 cups fresh basil, 1/2 cup parmesan, 1/2 cup olive oil, 1/3 cup pine nuts (or walnuts), and 2 garlic cloves in a food processor until smooth.
21. Fried Rice: Sauté diced onions, carrots, and peas. Push to side, scramble 2 eggs in the pan. Add cooked cold rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Toss everything together over high heat.
22. Chicken Parmesan: Bread chicken cutlets in flour, egg, then breadcrumbs. Fry until golden. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella. Bake at 400°F until cheese bubbles.
23. Classic Burger: Form ground beef into patties; season with salt and pepper. Grill or sear for 4 minutes per side. Top with cheese. Serve on a bun with lettuce, tomato, and condiments.
24. Mushroom Risotto: Sauté onions and arborio rice in butter. Add white wine and reduce. Slowly ladle in hot chicken broth, stirring constantly, until rice is creamy and tender. Finish with parmesan.
25. Roasted Chicken Thighs: Toss chicken thighs with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 35-40 minutes until skin is crispy.
26. Egg Salad: Hard boil 6 eggs. Peel and chop. Mix with 1/3 cup mayo, 1 tsp mustard, chopped chives, salt, and paprika.
27. BLT Sandwich: Toast bread. Spread with mayo. Layer crispy bacon, lettuce leaves, and salted tomato slices. Slice diagonally.
28. Macaroni and Cheese: Make a roux with equal parts butter and flour. Whisk in milk to make a béchamel. Melt in cheddar and gruyère cheese. Toss with cooked elbow macaroni.
29. Caesar Salad: Chop romaine lettuce. Toss with Caesar dressing, parmesan cheese, and croutons. Top with grilled chicken (optional).
30. Lentil Soup: Sauté onions, carrots, and celery. Add 1 cup dried lentils, 4 cups broth, cumin, and thyme. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until lentils are soft.
31. French Toast: Whisk 2 eggs, 1/2 cup milk, cinnamon, and vanilla. Dip thick bread slices into mixture. Fry in butter until golden brown on both sides.
32. Hummus: Blend 1 can drained chickpeas, 1/4 cup tahini, 1 garlic clove, lemon juice, olive oil, and cumin in a food processor until smooth.
33. Pork Chops: Season chops with salt, pepper, and rosemary. Sear in a skillet for 4 minutes per side. Finish in a 375°F oven for 5-8 minutes until cooked through.
34. Chicken Noodle Soup: Sauté carrots, celery, and onion. Add chicken broth and cooked shredded chicken. Bring to boil, add egg noodles, and cook until noodles are tender.
35. Bruschetta: Dice tomatoes and mix with minced garlic, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. Spoon onto toasted baguette slices rubbed with a raw garlic clove.
36. Chili: Brown ground beef with onions. Add chili powder, cumin, cayenne. Stir in canned kidney beans, diced tomatoes, and beef broth. Simmer for 1 hour.
37. Baked Sweet Potato Fries: Slice sweet potatoes into wedges. Toss with cornstarch, oil, paprika, and salt. Bake at 425°F (220°C) for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway.
38. Pasta Carbonara: Cook spaghetti. Fry pancetta/bacon. Whisk eggs and pecorino cheese. Toss hot pasta with pork fat, then remove from heat and quickly mix in egg mixture to create a creamy sauce (do not scramble).
39. Queso Dip: Melt 1 block of Velveeta cheese with 1 can of Ro-Tel (diced tomatoes and chilies) in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth.
40. Chicken Stir-Fry Marinade: Slice chicken breast. Marinate in soy sauce, cornstarch, and rice vinegar for 15 minutes before stir-frying for velvety texture.
41. Simple Meatballs: Mix 1 lb ground beef, 1 egg, 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, parmesan, parsley, and garlic. Form balls. Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes or simmer in sauce.
42. Deviled Eggs: Halve hard-boiled eggs. Mash yolks with mayo, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Pipe back into egg whites and dust with paprika.
43. Smoothie: Blend 1 banana, 1 cup frozen berries, 1 cup spinach, and 1 cup almond milk or yogurt until smooth.
44. Sheet Pan Sausage and Veggies: Toss sliced smoked sausage, bell peppers, onions, and zucchini with oil and Italian seasoning. Roast on a sheet pan at 400°F for 25 minutes.
45. Stuffed Peppers: Hollow out bell peppers. Mix cooked rice, ground beef, tomato sauce, and spices. Stuff peppers and bake at 375°F for 30-40 minutes. Top with cheese.
46. Chocolate Chip Cookies: Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt. Fold in chocolate chips. Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes.
47. Rice Krispie Treats: Melt 3 tbsp butter and 1 bag marshmallows. Stir in 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal. Press into a greased pan and let cool.
48. Mug Cake: Mix 4 tbsp flour, 4 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, 2 tbsp milk, 2 tbsp oil in a mug. Microwave for 90 seconds.
49. Fruit Salad: Chop watermelon, cantaloupe, strawberries, and grapes. Toss with a dressing of lime juice and honey. Mint garnish optional.
50. Popcorn (Stovetop): Heat 3 tbsp oil in a large pot with a lid. Add 1/2 cup kernels. Cover and shake continuously over medium-high heat until popping slows. Season with salt and butter.
